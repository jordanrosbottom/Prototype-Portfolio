Jordan Rosbottom | Graphic Design Portfolio

The Stay Safe Stay Home Campaign

The 'Stay Safe Stay Home' campaign flyer here is a mockup for a flu awareness flyer in order to get people to abide by CDC guidelines within the midst of flu season. I decided to use strong, simple graphics here to really drive home the point that in order to keep people safe, you need to stay home if you have symptoms.

Chip Kidd Tribute Poster

This work is a tribute to the inspirational graphic designer Chip Kidd. In this work I incorporated his iconic Jurrasic Park Logo style intertwined with his layout from his 'Watching the Watchmen' cover. Working through this project gave me newfound respect for one of the best designers I had yet to encounter at this point along my design journey.

Magazine Cover Mockup

This work is a mockup for a potential magazine cover. Incorporating the style of popular magazines like Vogue and SI, this project was an experiment in stylistic compatibility, very happy with the result and the experience gained from this project has made me a much better designer.

Alternate Book Cover

This is an alternate book cover for the Lois Lowry novel 'Number the Stars', a fantastic book that follows a young girl trying to help her Jewish friend escape the Nazi regim within Denmark. I chose to format the cover as I did because I wante dto really capture the essense of the novel, that being the important symbolism within the star of david necklace.

Logo Mockup

This work is a mock logo for a dessert company that ran specifically tailored Orange flavored ice cream. I decided th ebest course of action in order to create a logo that represented that product was to place an Orange right on top of the cone and bring it all together with text and other elements that made it stand out as a friendly brand.
